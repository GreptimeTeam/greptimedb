Expand description

Flushes the regions on the datanode.

Retry Or Ignore:

  • PusherNotFound, The datanode is unreachable.
  • Failed to flush region on the Datanode.

Abort:

  • MailboxTimeout, Timeout.
  • UnexpectedInstructionReply.
  • ExceededDeadline
  • Invalid JSON.
